Bora Bora

Day 1: Arrival and check-in. Relax at your overwater bungalow. Evening at Matira Beach.

Day 2: Snorkeling and scuba diving in Coral Gardens. Lunch on a beachside restaurant.

Day 3: Shark and ray feeding tour. Sunset cruise.

Popular Activities: Jet skiing, parasailing, hiking Mount Otemanu.

Cancun

Day 1: Check into hotel. Visit Playa Delfines. Explore the El Rey Maya ruins.

Day 2: Day trip to Chichen Itza and Cenote Ik Kil.

Day 3: Leisure day at Xcaret Park.

Popular Activities: Snorkeling, zip-lining, dolphin swims.

Phuket

Day 1: Arrival and visit Patong Beach. Nightlife at Bangla Road.

Day 2: Tour the Phi Phi Islands. Snorkeling and photography.

Day 3: Visit Big Buddha, Wat Chalong Temple. Thai massage experience.

Popular Activities: Sea kayaking, Muay Thai show, island hopping.

Barbados

Day 1: Explore Bridgetown. Visit Harrison's Cave.

Day 2: Snorkel with turtles in Carlisle Bay. Evening at Oistins Fish Fry.

Day 3: Relax at Bathsheba Beach. Visit the Mount Gay Rum Distillery.

Popular Activities: Surfing, catamaran sailing, historical tours.

Malibu

Day 1: Relax at Malibu Pier. Visit Zuma Beach.

Day 2: Explore the Getty Villa. Hike at Solstice Canyon.

Day 3: Malibu Wine Safari and relaxing on Paradise Cove Beach.

Popular Activities: Surfing, wine tasting, beach picnics.

Mykonos

Day 1: Arrival in Mykonos. Explore Mykonos Town.

Day 2: Day trip to Delos Island. Visit the Archaeological Museum of Mykonos.

Day 3: Relax at Psarou Beach. Sunset at Little Venice.

Popular Activities: Windmills of Kato Mili, Paradise Beach, nightlife.

Tahiti

Day 1: Arrival in Papeete. Explore the Municipal Market. Relax at your hotel.

Day 2: Island tour of Tahiti. Visit the Faarumai Waterfalls and the Museum of Tahiti and Her Islands.

Day 3: Snorkeling and swimming in the lagoon. Sunset cruise.

Popular Activities: Scuba diving, snorkeling, Polynesian cultural shows.
