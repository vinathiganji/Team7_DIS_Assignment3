Hawaii

Day 1: Explore Honolulu, Pearl Harbor, and Waikiki Beach.

Day 2: Tour of Volcanoes National Park.

Day 3: Beach relaxation and traditional Hawaiian Luau.

Popular Activities: Surfing, snorkeling, helicopter tours, hiking.

Maldives

Day 1: Check into a water villa, enjoy the ocean views.

Day 2: Snorkeling and optional scuba diving.

Day 3: Spa day and sunset cruise.

Popular Activities: Water skiing, jet skiing, diving, island hopping.

Seychelles

Day 1: Visit Victoria and Beau Vallon Beach on Mahe Island.

Day 2: Day trip to Praslin Island, visit Vallée de Mai.

Day 3: Bike tour around La Digue, Anse Source D’Argent.

Popular Activities: Snorkeling, sailing, fishing, nature walks.

Mauritius

Day 1: Visit Port Louis and Pamplemousses Botanical Garden.

Day 2: Black River Gorges National Park and Chamarel village.

Day 3: Catamaran cruise to nearby islands.

Popular Activities: Wind and kite surfing, hiking, quad biking.

Bali

Day 1: Arrival in Bali. Relax at your hotel or villa.

Day 2: Visit Ubud Monkey Forest, Tegalalang Rice Terraces.

Day 3: Explore Uluwatu Temple, enjoy a sunset Kecak dance.

Popular Activities: Surfing at Kuta Beach, snorkeling in Menjangan Island, visit Tanah Lot Temple.

Phuket

Day 1: Arrival and visit Patong Beach. Nightlife at Bangla Road.

Day 2: Tour the Phi Phi Islands. Snorkeling and photography.

Day 3: Visit Big Buddha, Wat Chalong Temple. Thai massage experience.

Popular Activities: Sea kayaking, Muay Thai show, island hopping.

Fiji

Day 1: Arrival at Nadi. Transfer to your resort. Relax and enjoy.

Day 2: Explore the Sabeto Hot Springs and Mud Pool. Visit Garden of the Sleeping Giant.

Day 3: Island-hopping tour to the Yasawa Islands. Snorkeling, swimming, and beach barbecue.

Popular Activities: Scuba diving, hiking in Taveuni, traditional Fijian cultural experience.
